Recommendations!!

I love playing games and these are my recommendations

  • Metal gear solid 3: it is about to get a rerelease and is a great introduction into one of the greatest game series ever made.
  • Doom (2016): It is a faithful reimagining of the classic series in a fun and action packed way that can bring anyone into the series
  • Marvel Spider-Man for the PS4: It is one of the most fun and innovative games on the Playstation systems and has a sequel already out and one coming out soon as well so now is the perfect time to get into the series.
  • Halo Master Chief Collection: This is an amazing steal you get all of the greatest halo games for the price of a single game. You can experience 6 different amazing story modes and their respective wonderful multiplayer modes.
These are my suggestions, they are all fairly basic but amazing picks I think everyone should try.
